    Adding Winamp skins
Adding Winamp skins to Audacious is very simple. Just copy your skin file
(.zip, .wsz, .tgz, .tar.gz, or .tar.bz2 file) to either
~/.local/share/audacious/Skins	(affect your user only) or
/usr/share/audacious/Skins	(affects every user), and then you can browse and select it from Skinned Interface tab in Preferences.
Another way is dragging the skin file directly into the list view of available skins.
